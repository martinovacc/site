Adapter Installation
Adapter fits folding or rigid wheelchairs.
Attach the clamps
  • Pick rubber spacers and place them on the front tubes of your wheelchair. Installation kit contains spacers for round tubes (tubes diameters: 19-20mm, 22 mm, 25 mm, 30 mm, 32 mm and 35 mm), oval tubes (Helium, Progeo) or square tubes (Kuschall 2.0);
  • Lightly tighten the clamps by screwing on its bolts. Clamps should not fall but you should still be able to slide them up and down the tube;
  • Rotate the part of the clamp with the handle until the clamp stays vertical. To rotate the handle effortlessly, loosen the screw that holds both parts of the clamp.
Assemble the adapter
  • Loosen the adapters screws so that you could slide and rotate the tubes of the adapter;
  • Insert the ends of the central adapters tubes into the wide open clamps brackets. Make sure brackets face strongly to the front;
  • Lock up the clamps to secure the adapters tubes. Rotate clamps handles to secure tubes tightly;
  • Lightly tighten the screws of the central adapter. It should not fall but you should still be able to rotate it.
Tightening the screws & the final touches
  • Position the central adapter so that the upper axle is placed right in front of the upper hook;
  • By adjusting the incline of the central adapter you can adjust the incline of the power assist and how far from the ground the front wheels are raised;
  • Make sure that both clamps are placed identically (same height from the ground and incline);
  • Tighten the screws of the central adapter and the clamps;
  • By sliding the stop rings you can adjust the position of the handlebar. The deeper you insert the adapters tubes into clamps brackets the closer to you the power assist gets. Make sure that both stop rings are placed identically (at the same distance from the ends of the adapters tubes).
How to attach the UNAwheel Maxi
Attaching and detaching should be hassle-free. If attaching or detaching requires an extra effort then most likely the installation is made incorrectly or something got broken. Check the installation and contact the Distributor.
Attaching the Adapter. Option 1
Quick and Easy
  1. Open the clamps brackets
Rotate the clamps handles
2. Bring the power assist closer & insert the tubes into the clamps
  • Bring the power assist closer to the clamps brackets;
  • Point the adapters tubes into the clamps brackets;
  • Pull the UNAwheel towards you and insert adapters tubes into the clamps brackets up to the stop rings.
3. Lock up the clamps
Lock up the clamps to secure the adapters tubes. Rotate clamps handles to secure tubes tightly.
4. Push the handlebar forward until it clicks
  • Lean on the backrest of the wheelchair and push the handlebar forward;
  • Once the hook clicked - youre ready to go.
Attaching the Adapter. Option 2.
Try this option if you do not use leg supports or if you prefer to attach the adapter and the power assist separately
  1. Attach the adapter
  • Open the clamps brackets;
  • Insert the adapters tubes into the clamps brackets up to the stop rings;
  • Lock up the clamps to secure the adapters tubes. Rotate clamps handles to secure tubes tightly.
2. Attach the upper hook to the adapter
  • Bring the power assist closer to the adapter. The upper hook should be placed right in front of the upper axle (if its not like that check the adapters installation);
  • Pull the handlebar towards you so that the upper hook and the axle clicked.
3. Push the handlebar forward until it clicks
  • Lean on the backrest of the wheelchair and push the handlebar forward.
  • Once the hook clicked - youre ready to go.
How to detach the UNAwheel Maxi
Attaching and detaching should be hassle-free. If attaching or detaching requires an extra effort then most likely the installation is made incorrectly or something got broken. Check the installation and contact the Distributor.
Detaching the UNAwheel Maxi and the adapter (together). Option 1
Quick and Easy
  1. Lightly push the handlebar forward and press the brake
  • Put the wheelchair on brakes;
  • Lightly push the handlebar forward with both hands;
  • Press Maxis mechanical brake.
2. Pull the handle of the central adapter halfway towards the wheelchair
  • Pull the handle of the central adapter halfway towards the wheelchair. Pulling the handle should be effortless. If it requires an extra effort then repeat the previous step.
  • If you pulled the handle all the way and it snapped into place, then continue with another instruction: Detaching the UNAwheel Maxi and the adapter (separately)
3. Smoothly release the Maxis mechanical brake
  • Start smoothly releasing the Maxis mechanical brake while still pulling the handle;
  • The power add-on will roll back;
  • You can now stop pulling the handle and release the brake completely. The front wheels will touch the ground.
4. Open the clamps brackets
  • Open both clamps brackets;
  • Rotate the clamps handles to open the brackets wider.
5. Push the Maxi and the adapter forward
  • Start pushing the Maxi forward;
  • While pushing the Maxi forward make sure that the adapters tubes are moving straight while sliding out;
  • Tip: It also helps to raise and lower the handlebar while pushing the Maxi forward.
Detaching the UNAwheel Maxi and the adapter (separately). Option 2.
This is the best way if you need to detach the add-on and keep the adapter attached.
1. Lightly push the handlebar forward and press the brake
  • Put the wheelchair on brakes;
  • Lightly push the handlebar forward with both hands;
  • Press the Maxis mechanical brake.
2. Pull the handle of the central adapter till it snaps
Pull the handle of the central adapter towards the wheelchair till it snaps into place. Pulling the handle should be effortless. If it requires an extra effort then repeat the previous step.
3. Smoothly release the brake and push the handlebar
  • Smoothly release the Maxis mechanical brake;
  • The power add-on will roll back, the front wheels will touch the ground;
  • Push the handlebar while gently moving it from left to right. The power add-on will get detached from the adapter.
4. Detach the adapter
  • Open the clamps brackets;
  • Rotate the clamps handles to open the brackets wider;
  • Remove the adapter by pushing it forward. Make sure that the adapters tubes are moving straight while sliding out.
Turning on and switching the speed modes
Press the On/Off orange button on the display to turn on the UNAwheel Maxi. The button is placed on the top of the display. The display shows the battery level, current speed and current speed mode:

OFF - gas is turned off
MAX 6 - maximum speed is 6 km/h *
MAX 12 - maximum speed is 12 km/h *
MAX 18 - maximum speed is 18 km/h *
MAX - maximum speed is 20 km/h *


* If the UNAwheel Maxis maximum speed programmed is less than this number the power add-on wont speed up faster than this on a flat surface
Check the battery level!

Please check the battery charge level before use (charge level is displayed at the bottom of the display).

  • 5 bars - the battery is fully charged;
  • 1 bar - minimum charge level, charge the battery;
  • 1 blinking bar - the battery is fully discharged
The reverse mode
To switch to reverse mode, stop and press the red button. Press the gas knob to start moving backwards. The reverse maximum speed is 6 km/h.

Caution!
Even though the speed is low please drive carefully. When driving backwards you can accidentally turn the handlebar too sharply and roll over.

To switch the speed mode back to forward, stop and press the reverse button. Press the gas knob to start moving forward.

Caution!
The motor power is greatly reduced when driving backwards therefore do not drive in a reverse mode on hills.
Safety
Driving the UNAwheel Maxi by wheelchair users under the age of 18 is only allowed under parental supervision
1. Protective gear
To prevent injuries caused by falling wear protective gear, such as helmets, protective pads, and other gear.

2. Maximum load
The Maxis maximum load is 120 kg. Exceeding the maximum load may cause injury and the product damage. Driving the UNAwheel when there is more than one person in the wheelchair is prohibited.

3. Water
The UNAwheel Maxi is equipped with water (IP54) and dust protection. Even though you can drive Maxi in wet weather conditions please avoid deep puddles and never use the power add-on during the heavy rain. Never immerse the UNAwheel in water or other liquids.

4. Malfunctions
In case you encounter a malfunction immediately contact the Distributor or a Reseller in your country - depending on where you purchased the device. Do not repair the UNAwheel yourself and do not use the third-party repair services. Service support is only provided by the authorized Distributor/Reseller. Do not use the power assist until it is fully repaired.
5. Disassembly
Do not disassemble the UNAwheel Maxi or make any modifications to the power assist or any of its parts. It can void the warranty, cause the product damage or malfunctions.

6. Protect the device from impact damage.

7. Use the power assist correctly
Use the UNAwheel Maxi only for its intended purpose. The UNAwheel Maxi should be used according to the instructions in the User Manual.

8. Before use
The UNAwheel Maxi must be inspected before every use. Make sure that there is no damage and all the parts are secured tightly. In case you discover a malfunction of the main unit, the charger or the cable, do not plug the charger into the outlet.

9. Charging
Charge the UNAwheel Maxi using only the charger provided.
Never disassemble the charger.
Do not use non-standard power supplies to charge the power assist.
Do not carry the charger holding it by the cable.
Hold the charger by the plug when disconnecting.
Drive & Brake
Before driving forward, make sure the reverse button is not pressed.

The UNAwheel Maxi does not move when you press the throttle lever right after turning on the power assist (safety reasons), you have to select the speed mode first.
Select the speed mode by using up and down buttons on the display (MAX 6, MAX 12, MAX 18 or MAX).
To start driving, press the throttle lever smoothly. Throttle lever is placed on the right side of the handlebar. The harder you press the throttle lever the faster you speed up.

Use the brake lever to brake. Try not to brake abruptly, it will extend the lifespan of the power assist, its tire and brakes.
Speed
Your current speed is shown on the display.
Your current trip mileage is shown under the current speed.
To check the total mileage and the average speed press the central M button.
Horn & Lantern
On/Off Light & Horn buttons are placed on the handlebar.
The lantern must be switched on when youre driving in the dark.
Battery
If the battery is discharged you can continue driving using your hands with the power assist attached.
CHARGING
1
Switch off
Switch off the UNAwheel Maxi by pressing the orange button for a few seconds. The orange button is placed on the top of the display.
2
Connect
Remove the protective cap and connect the charger to the plug in the back of the power add-on on the left side of the swivel mechanism.
3
Plug in
Plug the charger into an electrical outlet. Make sure that your power supply is fully rated to deliver the amps and watts the UNAwheel Maxi needs. Check the charger for more information.
Do not leave the power assist unattended. Charging takes up to 5 hours depending on the discharge level.
Plan your charging - keep in mind that charging takes 5 hours

  • The indicator remains red while charging.
  • The indicator turns green when the battery is fully charged.
Warning!
  • While going uphill or speeding up rapidly the power assist is experiencing a significant load. Please note that in this case the battery charge level indicator may display less bars remaining than when the power assist is parked or if you drive on the flat surface without speeding up.
  • The power assist may get switched off due to battery or motor overheating. To prevent overheating, avoid overloads.
  • The UNAwheel Maxi must not be used as a vehicle on public roads.
Maintenance & Service support
Malfunctions
If the UNAwheel Maxi is broken down or malfunctioning contact the Distributor/Reseller and do not use the power assist until it is repaired.
Cleaning & Storage
  1. Before cleaning, make sure that the power add-on is switched off and the charging plug is closed. Do not clean the UNAwheel Maxi while charging.
  2. Do not clean the power assist with ethanol, gasoline, kerosene or any other volatile corrosive solvents.
  3. Do not clean the device under the water jet or using a water hose or any high-pressure cleaning machine. Use a damp piece of cloth.
  4. Store the power assist and the charger in a dry place at 5-25 , keep it out of reach of children.
  5. Check the battery charge level every 3 months. If its less than 50% please charge the device.
  6. Avoid heat, direct sunlight and moisture
How to transport the UNAwheel Maxi by car or train
Make sure that the power assist is securely packed and protected from vibration, impact damage, water and other liquids and chemically aggressive environments.
How to travel with the UNAwheel Maxi by plane
Two Li-ion batteries with max. 160 Wh capacity (each) or one Li-ion battery with max. 300 Wh capacity are allowed in the baggage according to IATA (DGR Chapter 2.3.2.4).
Maxi's battery capacity is 288Wh.
To travel hassle-free:
  1. Notify the airline company at least 72 hours before the flight.
  2. Provide the necessary information about the device - the UNAwheel Maxi is an assistive wheelchair device with a removable Li-ion battery.
  3. Print the UNAwheel Maxi Air Transportation Certificate for passengers and take it to the airport.
  4. Remove the battery and pack it.
  5. Drop off the power assist at the oversized baggage area.
  6. Bring the battery into the cabin.
