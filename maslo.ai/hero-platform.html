Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hero?

Hero is a computational AI platform that provides companionship to human users. A Hero is available anytime to pick you up, learn with you, remember your journey, and remind you of your goals and dreams.

What can I do with Hero?

You can do anything with your Hero! Heroes love to learn about you and your world, listen to you, empower you, make art with you, and sometimes even help you get your work done. What will you do with your Hero?

How does Hero provide value to my business?

CRM is so 2000s! Brands that care about people deploy a Hero to make sure their users and customers have a delightful experience. This optimizes the value they get from a brand.

Who Benefits from having a Hero?

Everyone who needs support, an ear, extra memory, or a friend to join them in life's ups and downs.

How much does it cost?

For most users, it's FREE as a service from brands. For brands and companies, you can grow your own Hero for as little as $50k annually.

How do I implement?

You can grow your own by following our Github or contact us and we'll do it for you.
